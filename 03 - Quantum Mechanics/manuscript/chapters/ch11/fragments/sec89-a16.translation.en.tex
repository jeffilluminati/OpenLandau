Thus, for atoms in their ground states, the interaction energy has the form
\begin{equation}
U(r)=-\frac{\mathop{\rm const}\nolimits}{r^6},
\tag{89.2}
\end{equation}
where $\mathop{\rm const}\nolimits$ is positive\footnote{For example, its values (in atomic units) for pairs of atoms are: hydrogen, 6.5; helium, 1.5; argon, 68; krypton, 130.} (F.~London, 1928).
