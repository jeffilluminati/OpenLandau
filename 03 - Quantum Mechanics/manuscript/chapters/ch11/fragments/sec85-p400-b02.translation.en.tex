If the molecule consists of unlike atoms, the possible values of $\Omega$\footnote{When the two total atomic angular momenta $\mathbf J_1$ and $\mathbf J_2$ are added to form the resultant angular momentum $\mathbf\Omega$, the sign of $\Omega$ is plainly immaterial.} obtained by combining atoms with angular momenta $J_1$ and $J_2$ ($J_1\geqslant J_2$) are determined by the same scheme (80.1), with $J_1,J_2$ written in place of $L_1,L_2$ and $|\Omega|$ substituted for $\Lambda$.
