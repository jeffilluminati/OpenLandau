In particular, for $i=0$, the statistical weights vanish for the levels with even $K$ of the ${}^1\Sigma_u^-$ and ${}^1\Sigma_g^-$ terms, and for the levels with odd $K$ of the ${}^1\Sigma_g^+$ and ${}^1\Sigma_u^+$ terms. In other words, in the electronic states ${}^1\Sigma_u^-$ and ${}^1\Sigma_g^-$ there are no rotational states with even $K$, while in the states ${}^1\Sigma_g^+$ and ${}^1\Sigma_u^+$ there are no rotational states with odd $K$.
