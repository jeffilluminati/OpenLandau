It follows that the sign of the rotational components of a $\Sigma^+$ term is fixed by the factor $(-1)^K$: every level with even $K$ is positive, and every level with odd $K$ is negative. For a $\Sigma^-$ term, the sign of the rotational levels is fixed by $(-1)^{K+1}$; the levels with even $K$ are all negative, while those with odd $K$ are all positive.
