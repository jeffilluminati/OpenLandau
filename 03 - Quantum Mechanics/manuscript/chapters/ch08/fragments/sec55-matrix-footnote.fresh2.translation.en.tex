When matrices are written in the form (55.7), their rows and columns are indexed by the values of $\sigma$, with the row number corresponding to the first index of a matrix element and the column number to the second. Here these indices take the values $+1/2$ and $-1/2$. According to (55.4), applying the operator means multiplying the $\sigma$th row of the matrix by the wave-function components arranged as the column
$\displaystyle\psi=\binom{\psi(1/2)}{\psi(-1/2)}$.
