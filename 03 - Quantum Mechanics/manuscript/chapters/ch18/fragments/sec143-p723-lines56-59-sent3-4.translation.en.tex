Indeed, in the limit $k\to0$, the radial functions (for attraction) are given by (36.25), and near the center we have $R_{kl}\sim\sqrt{k}r^l$. The contribution from each individual $l$ to the square of the wave function in the reaction region is $\sim a^{2l}/k$, so all such contributions have the same dependence on $k$, although each is suppressed by the small factor $(a/a_c)^{2l}$ ($a_c=\hbar^2/(mZ_1Z_2e^2)$ --- the Coulomb unit of length).
