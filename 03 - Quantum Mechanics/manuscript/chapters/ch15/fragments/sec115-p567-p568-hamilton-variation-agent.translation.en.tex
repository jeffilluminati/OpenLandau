contains the term $(e/c)\mathbf v\mathbf A$.  When the charge is represented as
distributed in space, this term is instead written

changes the Lagrangian by
\[
 \delta L=\frac1c\int\mathbf j\,\delta\mathbf A\,dV.
\]
